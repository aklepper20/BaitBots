MY STORY

Its quite fitting to see myself as half robot, considering my left side is full of titanium due to my left sided spastic cerebral palsy.This design of myself is the result of many years of feeling the affects of the disability divide and an eternity of society's belief that my CP is my downfall. It took time and heartbreak to realize my CP is the sole driver to uncovering my superhuman powers. From conquering Hollywood, to teaching myself frontend code, I found that my calling is to program accessibilty applications. I hope to help individuals with disabilities become more independent and confident. By expanding education about disabilities and fighting for the proper resources to the path of equitable opportunities for all - we can close the disability divide and make this world a better place.

Aly Klepper
